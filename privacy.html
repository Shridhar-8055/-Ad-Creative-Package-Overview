Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Quantum Algo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, register for our webinars, or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register for our webinars or services:

  • Name - Your first name to personalize our communications
  • Email Address - To send webinar details, confirmation emails, and related updates
  • WhatsApp Number - To send webinar links and important reminders
  • Trading Experience Level - To tailor our training content to your needs

We also automatically collect certain technical information including your IP address, browser type, and device information through standard web server logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • To register you for our free webinars and live training sessions
  • To send confirmation emails with webinar meeting links and details
  • To send reminders about upcoming sessions via email or WhatsApp
  • To communicate updates about our courses, workshops, and educational content
  • To improve our services and tailor content to our audience
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your registration data is stored securely in Google Sheets and processed through Google Apps Script. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Google Workspace - For data storage, email sending, and webinar management
  • Google Meet - For hosting live webinar sessions
  • Vercel - For website hosting

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

5. Email Communications

By registering for our webinar, you consent to receiving:

  • Webinar confirmation and meeting link emails
  • Reminder emails about upcoming sessions
  • Follow-up emails with resources and additional training opportunities

You can opt out of promotional emails at any time by replying with "UNSUBSCRIBE" or contacting us directly. Transactional emails (such as registration confirmations) are necessary and cannot be opted out of.

6. Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. No personal data is collected through cookies on our site.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your personal data from our records
  • Opt-out - Unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
