Creative Guidelines

🎨 Color Psychology

Red - Pain points, urgency, losses
Green - Solutions, profits, success
Yellow/Gold - CTAs, attention, premium
Purple/Blue - Tech, AI, trust
Black - Premium, serious, professional

🖋 Typography

Headlines: 64-72px Bold
Body: 42-52px Medium
CTAs: 56-64px Bold

All text must be readable on 6-inch phone screen

🎮 Testing Strategy

  • Pain vs. Opportunity messaging
  • Price-focused vs. Value-focused
  • Student results vs. Your results
  • Static vs. Video
  • Short copy vs. Long copy
  • Urgency vs. No urgency
